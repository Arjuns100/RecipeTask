PANCAKES RECIPE



INGREDIENTS
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tabelspoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tabelspoons melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
COOKING STEPS
  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour,sugar, baking powder,baking soda and salt.
  2. In another bowl, mix the milk,egg,melted butter and vanilla extract until well combined.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, stirring gently until just combined. A few lumbs are okay.
  4. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease with butter.
  5. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface(about 2-3 minutes), then flip and cook for an additonal 1-2 minutes until golden brown.
  7. Serve warm with your favorite toppings, like syrup, berries or whipped cream.
Enjoy your homemade pancakes!
